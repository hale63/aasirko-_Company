GLOBAL NETWORK

Western European Network.
Beyond comparison.

We continue to use our logistical strength to expand our presence across the Netherlands, Belgium, Luxembourg, and Germany. Our Western European hub ensures fast, secure, and efficient distribution to areas where clinical trials and patient populations are increasing. It is all about location. We welcome you to explore our network to reach your patients faster.

🇳🇱 Netherlands

Nederland — Fast nationwide coverage

Our Dutch operations form the backbone of our European logistics. With access to Rotterdam — Europe's largest port — we ensure rapid, reliable nationwide distribution and seamless international connections.

🇧🇪 Belgium

België — Reliable cross-border delivery

Belgium's central position in Western Europe makes it a critical node in our network. We deliver reliably across all Belgian regions and leverage its infrastructure to connect neighbouring markets with speed and precision.

🇱🇺 Luxembourg

Strategic logistics hub

Luxembourg serves as a vital strategic hub within our network. Its prime location at the crossroads of France, Belgium, and Germany enables swift and efficient distribution throughout the region.

🇩🇪 Germany

Duitsland — Strong transport connections

Germany's extensive road, rail, and air network amplifies our reach across Central Europe. Our German operations ensure consistent delivery performance and support time-sensitive pharmaceutical logistics at scale.

🌍 Western Europe

Beyond our core markets, Aasirko Global maintains broad Western European coverage. Our flexible logistics model adapts to the unique demands of each territory, ensuring no patient population is out of reach.
