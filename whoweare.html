Logistics is not about moving boxes — it's about keeping promises. Every shipment we handle carries someone's trust, someone's livelihood, someone's future. That responsibility has driven everything we have built at AA-SİRKO for the past 28 years.

Wael founded AA-SİRKO with a single truck and an unwavering belief that transparency and reliability could transform the logistics industry. Under his leadership, the company has grown into a pan-European and Middle Eastern freight network serving over 120 countries, while maintaining the personal accountability of a family-owned business.

With a background in international trade law and supply chain engineering, he brings a rare combination of legal precision and operational instinct to every strategic decision. He is a frequent speaker at the World Logistics Forum and serves on the advisory board of the European Freight & Logistics Council.
